## Interpretation Notes

* Z-direction response amplitudes include the static disk weight in the rotating force vector (kept for parity with the original MATLAB model), so Z curves carry the static sag in addition to vibration; Y curves show the pure unbalance response.
* The model is linear: amplitudes at resonance peaks can exceed the bearing radial clearance and are resonance indicators, not physically attainable displacements.
