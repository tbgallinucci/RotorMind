## Rotordynamics / Bearing Theory

* [Journal Bearing Theory](journal-bearing-theory.md) - Hydrodynamic journal bearings: oil-film load carrying, eccentricity ratio and attitude angle, linearized stiffness and damping coefficients, cross-coupling, oil whirl and oil whip, ball bearings by contrast.
* [Reynolds Equation](reynolds-equation.md) - Governing equation of hydrodynamic lubrication: assumptions, wedge and squeeze terms, film thickness, short-bearing (Ocvirk) closed-form forces used by the engine, load-capacity scaling.

## Rotordynamics / Standards and Practice

* [Critical Speeds and Separation Margin](critical-speeds-and-separation-margin.md) - Critical speeds, Campbell diagrams, and amplification factor by the half-power method, summarized in original words.
* [API 610 - Lateral Analysis Requirements (5.2.4 / Appendix I)](api-610-lateral-analysis.md) - The binding two-step pipeline: is a lateral analysis even required (classically-stiff screen), and if so, does it pass the Appendix I separation-margin/damping, allowable-displacement, and shop-verification criteria.

## Rotordynamics / Reference

* [Rotordynamics Glossary](rotordynamics-glossary.md) - Working definitions: critical speed, whirl, gyroscopic effect, unbalance, eccentricity ratio, stiffness and damping coefficients, mode shape, FRF, amplification factor, separation margin.
